Bo,

An emergency landed spaceship for your break.

Is it challenging to take a break during the home office? What would it be if an alien landed at your house for the first time and asks you for guidance?


Feb.2021
Germany
Experiment


"Bo," is an alien hiding in a spaceship looks like a robot. He runs around everywhere and take a journey through your room or the entire apartment. After 25 minutes he stops, takes a photo and sends it to you. He asks you, “Bo, bo, bo… bo(wo : where) am I ?". Now starts Hide and Seek, you have to look for him and find him with the Hints from Bo's photos. Otherwise your screen will remain locked. You will be away from your work screen until you find Bo,. Have a playful break!
